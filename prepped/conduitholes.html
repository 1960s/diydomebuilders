MAKING HOLES

In this article we are going to look at how to make holes in struts for a conduit dome.

Making Holes

-
How do I get holes into my tubing/pipe/conduit?

I'll start with the most primitive methods and end with complex tools.

Manual Hand Drill
Hand Drill
Drill Press
Sledgehammer + Punch
Arbor Press + Punch Die
Punch Press

Whatever tool you decide to use, you will need something to hold the conduit in place while you are cutting.
The conduit will move around if it isn't keep still and will roll more than anything.
There is a method that will make everything a whole lot easier. It involves flattening one end first, then placing it into a crevice. Or leave it in the press after you've flattened, then cut it. Miles easier.


Summary

  • Use steel bolts, zinc for normal wear and hot-dipped for coastal weathering.
  • Use flange nuts and bolts for putting your dome frame together.
  • Use hex nuts and washers for your dome cover. Washers can prevent the cover for tearing in tough weather.
