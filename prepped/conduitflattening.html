This is where using a pipe cutter over hacksaw is better.

The pipe cutter guarentees you a clean and straight cut even from a small marking.

Holding the Conduit In Place


You'll find that when trying to make a cut, it may difficult to do without the conduit moving.

A simple way of keeping the conduit in place is to flatten one end first.

Then place the flattened end in a crevice, vice, or press to prevent it from moving.



Summary

  • Your most affordable tools are going to be either the hacksaw or the pipe cutter.
  • The pipe cutter is the recommended tool for cutting conduit.
  • Use hex nuts and washers for your dome cover. Washers can prevent the cover for tearing in tough weather.
